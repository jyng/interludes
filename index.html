interludes

a bi-weekly newsletter of small stories

Our generation holds Top 40 songs dearly because they celebrate large moments in our lives: New Years, Spring Break, The 21st Birthday, Graduation. But what marks the times in between? Do you remember the morning of your last final before graduating? The shitty bacon hot dog at 4 a.m. on your 21st birthday? If major songs represent the milestones of your life, then interludes are the pebbles that bring you there. We want to pick up the pebbles and showcase their beauty. We want to celebrate these smaller moments, because there's just so much noise otherwise. Please join us in our curiosity.
