interludes

a biweekly newsletter of small stories

Whenever we look back on life, we can always spot the big moments. The college graduation. The 21st birthday. The move into a new city. But we often let the smaller moments slip. We lose them in fanfare of the bigger moments. The frivolous days before graduation. The short dinner after moving into the new apartment. Do we remember these moments?

Interludes focuses on people, places, and things that have slipped. We want to prove that these smaller stories can hold their own embers, too. But really, we just hope that these stories will make you smile.
